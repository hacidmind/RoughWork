Buffalo Sausage Cheese Dip

List of Ingredients:

  • 1 package Turkey Sausage Crumbles
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, cubed, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup Louisiana brand hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup (2 ounces) blue cheese crumbles
  • Baked pita chips or celery sticks for dipping

Steps:

  1. Heat and stir sausage crumbles in a large skillet over medium heat for 4-5 minutes or until hot. Add cream cheese, sour cream and hot sauce.
  2. Cook and stir until cream cheese is melted and sauce is smooth. Stir in blue cheese and celery; heat 2 minutes or until cheese is melted. Spoon into serving dish or chafing dish.
  3. Serve with chips and celery sticks.

Sausage Bread Bowl

List of Ingredients:

  • 1 package Original Sausage Crumbles
  • 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies, drained
  • 1 lb. round bread loaf

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350⁰F.
  2. Combine sausage, cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream and green chilies in a large bowl until well blended.
  3. Cut slice from top of bread loaf; remove center, leaving 1-inch-thick shell. Cut removed bread into bite-sized pieces. Spoon sausage mixture into bread bowl.
  4. Place bread bowl on baking sheet. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until hot. Serve with bread and crackers, if desired.

Sausage and Cheese Calzone

List of Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon instant dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 cup warm water (approx. 105 degrees F)
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Filling
  • 1 package Jimmy Dean© Premium Pork Sausage
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced

Steps:

  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine yeast, brown sugar, salt, water, olive oil, and flour. Knead on low until dough starts to come together. Continue kneading until the dough forms a nice, soft dough ball and the sides of the bowl are clean.
  2. Let rise in a lightly greased bowl, covered, for 1 hour, until doubled in size.
  3. Meanwhile, make the filling. Heat a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Add in Jimmy Dean Premium Pork Regular Sausage and cook 5 minutes. Use a spatula to break up the sausage into crumbles. After 5 minutes, add in the bell peppers and mushrooms. Continue to saute until vegetables are tender crisp and sausage is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll into circles with a 12-inch diameter. Spoon the sausage mixture onto one half of the dough, leaving a 1 inch edge. Top with 1/2 cup cheese per calzone. Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges.
  5. Transfer stuffed calzones to a lightly greased baking sheet. Use a sharp knife to make three slits in the top of each calzone to allow steam to vent.
  6. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes until bread is golden brown. Serve hot.
